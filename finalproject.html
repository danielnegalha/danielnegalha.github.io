Final Project

My final project, Don't Lose, is a challenging and fun game coded on Khan Academy. The object of the game is to make it to the other end of each level while dodging fast projectiles. The game uses the arrow keys for movement and is very fun to play. After my partner and I wrote around 650 lines of code, we finished this super enjoyable game. Although frustrating, I challenge you to attempt to beat it. I included a link to the game, and a video describing the gameplay below. Have fun, and remember, Don't Lose!
